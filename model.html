Honda SP125 Expert Review

BikeDekho Experts

Shinier Shine!

The 2025 Honda SP125 is basically a sportier version of India’s best selling 125cc bike - the Shine 125. Unlike the Shine, which has a very commuter-focussed design, the SP125 gets edgier body panels and a lot more features. In fact, the SP125 was among the first few 125cc bikes in India that got sporty styling - making it completely stand apart from other bikes in the market.
